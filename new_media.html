Glitch Art

With a new given perspective, you can witness the familiar with fresh eyes and experience it in a way you never thought possible. Creatures and Environment

Medium: Zbrush, Blender

During the course of this project, I had the opportunity to delve into the world of 3D medium, where I was able to exercise my creative muscles and explore my artistic abilities by designing and applying various facial features to each head.

For this project, I aimed to provide a glimpse into an exciting future where technology reigns supreme, offering a unique perspective on what it might be like to navigate a world dominated by our electronic creations.
