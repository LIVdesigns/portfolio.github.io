Drawings and Sketches

I have experimented with a wide range of mediums, each offering its own unique set of challenges and opportunities for creative expression. Among these mediums, I have utilized various techniques, such as charcoal, negative space, pencil, and marker.

Words of Freedom

Materials: Air dry clay, paint, water, sharpies, invisible string, glue, newspaper, shoebox, plaster

By utilizing these materials, I was able to produce a work of art that symbolizes freedom through the power of language. Through the act of releasing origami paper cranes with open hands, the piece conveys the notion that words can inspire and disseminate widely with ease.
