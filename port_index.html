Artist Statement

I have always gravitated toward bold colors, vibrancy and animation. I found that graphic design emanates all three attributes. Designing online makes me feel as though I can capture or create strong, inspirational pieces with energy and movement. Web design allows me to open a new platform to express my personality and further push my abilities that can one day be an inspiration to others.
